Designed for Accessibility

An AI-powered application built to help visually impaired individuals identify Indian currency notes independently.

Our Purpose

Millions of visually impaired people face a daily challenge — identifying their currency. Asking someone else compromises independence and privacy.

CurrencySense uses your phone's camera and deep learning to instantly detect and speak aloud the denomination of any Indian currency note.

How It Works

01

Open & Scan

Tap the Scan button. Your camera activates automatically.

02

Point the Note

Hold any Indian note in front of your camera. The AI analyzes multiple frames.

03

Hear the Result

The app speaks the denomination aloud — completely hands-free.

Capabilities

7 Denominations

₹10, ₹20, ₹50, ₹100, ₹200, ₹500, ₹2000.

Voice Output

Text-to-speech announces the result instantly.

Camera-Based

No special hardware. Any standard smartphone.

Multi-Frame Analysis

Majority voting for confident predictions.

User Accounts

Secure personal accounts with admin oversight.

Deep Learning

CNN for high accuracy across lighting conditions.
